Know Your City
AI-Powered Neighborhood Safety & Insights Platform

Hackathon Submission — TEAM WINGS
Team Leader: Aryan Kothari

Overview

Know Your City is a platform designed to help newcomers relocate to Indian cities safely and confidently. It provides real-time neighborhood safety scores, family-friendly information, and local amenities insights, all powered by AI and verified data.

Problem Statement

Relocating to a new city can be challenging:

Safety information is scattered and unreliable
Finding family-friendly neighborhoods is difficult
Access to schools, hospitals, parks, and public transport is unclear
Newcomers lack localized guidance

These challenges can lead to unsafe decisions and stress for residents and families.

Solution

Know Your City addresses these challenges by offering:

Real-time safety scores for neighborhoods
Ratings for family-friendliness and walkability
Proximity to schools, hospitals, parks, and public transit
Verified local tips and amenities

This platform allows newcomers to make data-driven decisions about relocation and daily life in a new city.

Key Features
Neighborhood Safety Explorer: Provides safety scores for all neighborhoods
Schools and Parks: Displays nearby family-friendly options
Transport Access: Highlights proximity to metro, bus stops, and major routes
Hospitals and Emergency Services: Shows nearby hospitals and police stations
Safe Route Finder: Generates the safest paths within the city
Local Tips: Offers insights from community-verified sources
AI Integration
Neighborhood Safety Score: Combines data on police presence, hospitals, fire stations, and CCTV coverage
Personalized Recommendations: Suggests safe zones, schools, parks, and essential amenities
Real-Time Routing: Calculates safest routes based on live location data
Data Aggregation: Integrates OpenStreetMap data with verified local information
