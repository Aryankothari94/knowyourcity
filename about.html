🌃 Who We Are

Know Your City started with a simple observation: relocating is stressful, primarily because of the unknown safety and amenity landscape of a new city. Our platform bridges this gap by aggregating realtime urban infrastructure data into a simple, actionable safety score.

We are a team of urban planners, data scientists, and safety advocates dedicated to making India's cities transparent and accessible to everyone — from solo professional women to young families.

🎯 Our Vision

Our vision is to become the universal safety layer for urban living. We want to empower newcomers with verified, genuine data about school proximity, healthcare availability, and emergency services density.

Authenticity We rely on verified OSM and public infrastructure records.
Transparency Our scoring algorithms are clearly explained and data-backed.
Accessibility Safety information should be free and accessible to all.
Community We leverage local insights and stories to enrich our data.
