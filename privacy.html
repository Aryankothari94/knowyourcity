🛡️ Privacy First

Transparency Protocol

Last updated: March 26, 2026

🔍 Data We Collect

Your safety shouldn't come at the cost of your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with neighborhood insights:

  • Account Info: Name and email to personalize your experience.
  • Location Data: To show safety scores for your specific area.
  • Preferences: Cities you explore to improve our mapping algorithms.
🔒 Note: We never sell your personal data. Your location history is anonymized and used only for regional safety analysis.

🔋 How We Use Data

We use the data collected to:

  • Calculate and personalize safety scores for your neighborhood.
  • Identify zones where community feedback indicates a need for more data.
  • Improve Safe Route Finder accuracy based on common transit routes.
  • Notify you of critical safety updates in your saved areas.

🤝 Third Party Services

We work with trusted partners to deliver our mapping services:

  • OpenStreetMap: Our primary source for infrastructure nodes.
  • Wikipedia: For providing historical and cultural context for cities.
  • Leaflet/Esri: For high-performance interactive map rendering.

These services may collect anonymized browser data according to their own privacy policies.

🔑 Your Rights

You have full control over your data on Know Your City:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data at any time.
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data instantly.
  • Opt-out: Disable location tracking from your browser settings.

For any privacy-related requests, contact us at privacy@knowyourcity.in.
