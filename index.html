How It Works

Your Journey in 5 Simple Steps

From setting your location to exploring safe neighborhoods — here's exactly how to use Know Your City like a pro.

1
location_on

Set Your Location

Click the location badge in the top-right corner of the navbar. Search for your city or use GPS auto-detection. Your dashboard instantly personalizes to your area.

vpn_key Pro Tip

You can change your city anytime. All safety maps, analytics, and recommendations update in real-time when you switch.

2
map

View the Safety Heatmap

Scroll to the Neighborhood Safety Analysis section. The satellite map shows a color-coded grid based on police, hospitals, and CCTV coverage.

analytics What You See

Green = high safety coverage, Orange = moderate, Red = low coverage. Each grid cell is analyzed within an 8km radius.

3
search

Search Any Neighborhood

Use the Safety Explorer search bar to type any area. Get instant safety scores, family-friendliness ratings, walkability metrics, and Wikipedia analytics.

auto_awesome Feature

Search auto-suggests real locations as you type. Results include nearby tourist attractions with verified Wikipedia descriptions.

4
shield

Explore Safe Routes & Zones

Click "Safe Zone" or "Safe Route Finder" from the features panel. Navigate with satellite maps, real-time routing, and journey tracking.

rocket_launch Bonus

Route finder includes "Cancel Journey" toggle, satellite/dark map layers, and live GPS tracking during your commute.

5
home

Decide & Settle In

Compare neighborhoods side-by-side, review Pro Tips from our analytics engine, browse nearby schools and parks, and confidently choose where to live.

star Result

A complete, data-backed picture of your new neighborhood — safety, amenities, culture, and commute — before you ever step foot there.
